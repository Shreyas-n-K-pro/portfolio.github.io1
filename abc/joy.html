Data Structures and Algorithms – Fantasy Learning

What kind of Data Structures have you studied?

  • Arrays (like lists of items)
  • Stacks (like a stack of plates)
  • Queues (like a line of people)
  • Linked Lists (like a chain)
  • Advanced ones like Trees (family trees)
  • Graphs (maps with points and connections)

What kind of Algorithms have you studied?

  • Sorting and Searching: Organizing data (e.g., sorting numbers) or quickly finding items (e.g., a name in a contact list).
  • Pathfinding: Finding the shortest route, like Google Maps.
  • Optimization: Making the best choices, like packing a bag efficiently with limited space.

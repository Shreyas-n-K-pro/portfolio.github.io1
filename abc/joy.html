Course Project Introduction

The course project focuses on addressing real-world problem spaces by leveraging data structures and algorithms. The problem space was identified through in-depth research, brainstorming, and findings summarized from the white paper.

Reflecting on city design, the team identified business cases such as route optimization, data visualization, and resource management. The project integrates these into a cohesive framework, providing solutions tailored to real-time scenarios.

Business Cases

  • Shreyas: Optimizing routes for delivery services using graph-based algorithms.
  • Yashavanth: Analyzing social networks with graph traversal techniques.
  • Suleman: Inventory management with efficient searching and sorting algorithms.
  • Jayathirth: Visualizing city traffic data using tree-based data structures.
